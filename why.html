Why VSI ?

  • A new wave of learning, a new world order, a brand-new perspective in schooling.
  • Why stay boxed in, within the parameters of mandatory and limited subjects, rigid school hours, travelling pressures and safety concerns?
  • With VSI, now you can have the best of both worlds within your reach. Welcome to Virtual School, the learning portals of the future!
  • How is VSI different from other schools? In a myriad ways.

Right Balance

VSI believes that "A free mind leads to unfettered imagination". Thus we provide a striking balance between scholastic and co-scholastic arenas for the holistic development of the child.

Individual Attention

We are committed to maintaining an extremely healthy and productive student teacher ratio for every class. Whats more, teachers are handpicked to teach subjects/ chapters and topics are their strong suits. So, not only does every child get individual attention but they are also taught by experienced and proficient staff best suited for every topic.

Choice of subjects

At VSI, students that opt for NIOS certification can choose subjects best suited to their interests.

Caters to Advanced & Slow Learners With Mitigates Travel, Safety and Health Concerns

Children have different understanding, grasping and assimilating paces. Some are quick and highly advances vis--vis their age while some others may be slow on the uptake. By offering a flexible and simple curriculum like NIOS, at Srpuha we can play to each childs core strength, thereby spurring them on to excel in whatever they opt for and at their pace.

Caters to Achievers in Sports and Extra-Curriculars With No Need For Additional Tuition or Extra Classes

VSI's flexible curriculum enables the students to learn at their pace and develop the 21st century skills during their journey at VSI. VSI provides a hazzle free quality learning environment at the safety and comfort of your home. VSI enables the students to do away with the paranoia of exposing oneself to health & safety concerns like traffic and pollution.

Competitive Fee Structure

At VSI, we believe everyone can and should have access to quality education without having to burn a hole through their pockets. Our Virtual model of schooling allows us to charge extremely competitive fees by saving on regular infrastructure costs.
