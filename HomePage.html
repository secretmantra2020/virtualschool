Our Virtual School Experience
Access an outstanding education and world-class teaching through our Virtual School
Learn More

We put your child’s learning first. Our Virtual School Experience means access to an outstanding education and world-class teaching always. Delivering high-quality lessons, our virtual school connects your child to their classmates, teachers, and unique global opportunities for truly personalized learning.

Our creative and innovative learning approach strikes a balance between online and offline learning time so that your child can make the most of personalized learning from home.

What you can expect for your child
Virtual Learning for Early Years

Our Early Years virtual school is designed to engage young minds with interactive teaching and exploratory activities. Inspiring lessons from our teachers will ensure your child's day is an engaging one. Your Early Years’ child will:

  • Take part in real-time lessons with their world-class teachers through our safe & secure online platform
  • Develop essential skills and nurture curiosity through independent learning and play
  • Have fun trying new activities online and offline

Your child’s progress will be assessed, and you will receive regular updates from their teacher on their growth.

Virtual Learning for Primary & Secondary

Every virtual primary or secondary school day is an engaging one that will inspire your child with innovative lessons, unique learning opportunities, and high-quality teaching as part of our blended learning approach. Our Virtual School Experience has two key aspects:

  • Connected time where students can learn from their teachers ‘live’ using our safe and secure online platform
  • Guided time where students learn independently through tailored tasks set by their teacher

Global Campus

Global Campus brings an array of new and exciting learning activities ranging from Story Sacks and MIT STEAM challenges to Expeditions, Writers Corner, and more. These activities designed by experts, support students from ages 2-18:

  • Through Global Campus, your child will immerse in international projects and challenges, while on the other hand, gets a better understanding of what school life is like in other countries
  • The access to one-of-a-kind learning activities from our exclusive collaborations with MIT, UNICEF and Juilliard helps develop students' Creativity, Collaboration, Courage, Critical Thinking, Cultural Awareness and Communication
  • While it encourages students to discuss, collaborate, compete, and celebrate with one another, it also provides your child with an extensive range of learning opportunities 24/7/365
